\lettersection{About Me}
I am Abhishek Das, a recent MCA graduate with a background in Python, SQL, and data reporting. Over the past year, I have worked in two internships where I handled real data problems — from analyzing supply chain data at Sukoon Unlimited, which led to \$250,000 in annual cost savings, to building Power BI dashboards used by 30+ team members at Unified Mentor. I have also built personal projects including a churn prediction model (87\% accuracy) and a transport delay prediction system (85\% accuracy). These experiences have given me a practical understanding of how data moves, gets cleaned, and gets used in decisions.

\lettersection{Why <<COMPANY_NAME>>?}
I am applying to <<COMPANY_NAME>> because the scale and type of data problems here match what I want to work on next. I want to move from analysis and reporting into building the pipelines and systems that make that analysis possible. From what I have read about <<COMPANY_NAME>>, the data infrastructure work here is serious and team-driven, which is exactly the kind of environment where I can grow and contribute at the same time.

\lettersection{Why Me?}
I have spent the last year getting close to data — cleaning it, modeling it, and building reports on top of it. I understand what happens when data quality is poor or pipelines are unreliable, because I have had to fix those problems firsthand. I write clean SQL, I am comfortable with Python for data tasks, and I pick up new tools quickly. I am not coming in with years of production pipeline experience, but I am coming in with a clear understanding of data workflows and a habit of figuring things out. I want to build things that work reliably, and I take that seriously.

\end{cvletter}
